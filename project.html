Student Schedule Tracker System

A student schedule tracker system is a customizable app that helps students organize and track their class schedules. It allows students to input and view their subjects, along with the days and times they occur, providing a clear overview of their weekly academic commitments. The system is flexible, enabling students to adjust the layout and details to suit their preferences. This tool helps students stay organized and manage their time effectively, without the need for notifications or additional features like exams.
